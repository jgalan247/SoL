Welcome to Advanced Level!

Congratulations on reaching the advanced level! These challenging projects combine everything you've learned and introduce sophisticated concepts. You'll create complex systems, work with multiple Micro:bits, and build projects that could be used in the real world.

Skills You'll Master:

  • Complex multi-device systems
  • Advanced sensor integration
  • Creating reusable functions and modules
  • State machines and complex logic
  • Real-world applications
  • Project design and planning
  • Debugging complex programs

Prerequisites

These projects require strong understanding of all previous concepts including: radio communication, external components, functions, lists, timing, and game logic. Complete intermediate projects first!

Materials You May Need

  • Multiple Micro:bits (2-3 recommended)
  • External speakers or headphones with crocodile clips
  • Crocodile clip leads
  • Various craft materials for enclosures
  • Optional: servo motors, additional sensors
